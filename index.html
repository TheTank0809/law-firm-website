About

A Lawyer Who Fights
For What Matters

Advocate Pallavi Srivastava is a seasoned litigator practising at the High Court of Karnataka, Bengaluru. With over a decade of hands-on courtroom experience, she brings deep expertise across corporate disputes, intellectual property, civil litigation, constitutional challenges, and media law.

Her practice is built on three pillars — rigorous preparation, honest counsel, and relentless advocacy. Whether you are a startup navigating insolvency proceedings or an individual facing a wrongful eviction, she gives every matter the focus it deserves.
